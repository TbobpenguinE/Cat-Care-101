Cat Basics

This page covers the basic needs of most indoor cats: food, water, litter, playtime, and rest.

Every cat is different, but these general guidelines are a good starting point for keeping your cat comfortable.

Daily Routine

A predictable routine helps many cats feel calm and safe.

  1. Morning feeding and clean water.
  2. Quick play session.
  3. Evening feeding and litter scoop.

Environment

Cats like to climb, scratch, and look out windows. Try to give them safe ways to do all three.

  • Cat tree or shelves
  • Sturdy scratching post
  • Window perch or safe sill

Health & Safety

Indoor cats can live long, healthy lives with regular care.
